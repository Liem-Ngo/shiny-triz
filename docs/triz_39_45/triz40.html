About TRIZ Principles

The 40 TRIZ principles are problem-solving strategies derived from the Theory of Inventive Problem Solving (TRIZ).

These principles represent patterns of innovation that can be applied across industries to solve technical contradictions.

How to use:

  1. Select a principle from the dropdown menu
  2. View the general description and examples
  3. Choose an industry to see specialized examples

TRIZ (the Theory of Inventive Problem Solving) is a systematic approach to innovation that helps identify and solve technical contradictions.

At the heart of TRIZ are 40 inventive principles that represent patterns observed across thousands of patents and innovations.

This tool allows you to explore these principles and see how they can be applied in different industries and contexts.
