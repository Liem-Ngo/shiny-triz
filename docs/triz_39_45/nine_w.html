Thinking Framework Guidelines
  • Super System: Focus on the broader environment, market trends, regulatory factors, or societal changes that affect your system.
  • System: Analyze the main subject of interest - could be a product, organization, process, or technology.
  • Subsystem: Examine the components, features, or elements that make up the system.
  • Past → Present → Future: Identify patterns of evolution to make informed predictions about future developments.
